ABOUT US

Our Story

Confidence Supplies Ltd is a leading provider of high-quality industrial supplies and services in Kenya and beyond. Established with a vision to be the trusted partner for businesses seeking reliable and efficient solutions, we have grown to offer an extensive range of products and expert services. From our humble beginnings, we have consistently prioritized customer satisfaction, innovation, and unwavering quality, building lasting relationships and contributing to the industrial growth of the region. Our journey is marked by a relentless pursuit of excellence and a deep understanding of our clients' evolving needs.

We specialize in a diverse portfolio including steam products, factory machinery, general supplies, steam installations, plumbing solutions, and industrial machinery services. Our commitment extends beyond just supply; we aim to provide comprehensive solutions that enhance operational efficiency and sustainability for our clients.

OUR MISSION

To consistently deliver superior quality industrial products and expert services, fostering long-term partnerships through reliability, innovation, and unparalleled customer satisfaction.

OUR VISION

To be the most trusted and preferred industrial supply and service provider in East Africa, recognized for our comprehensive solutions, technical expertise, and commitment to sustainable industrial growth.
